Six Hats Engine

Submit your problem statement and idea to the Hats; afterwards, check in with each each Hat individually to hear each agent's perspective. The Blue Hat will remember everything that has been said so far, and will provide a big-picture summary of all existing perspectives.

The problem statement should be a mission that your design or product idea has been created to address; e.g., "improving the wellbeing of people who work in a mobile environment".

White Hat

an analytical agent focused only on providing facts without judgement: information we know, information we need to know, and technical details

the analytical agent

Red Hat

focused on the emotional perspective: empathizing with the fears, likes, dislikes, loves, and hates of the intended user

the emotional agent

Black Hat

a pessimistic, critical agent who is concerned with risks, difficulties, and problems

the pessimistic agent

Yellow Hat

an optimistic, positive agent who looks at the positive aspects and probes for value and benefit

the optimistic agent

Green Hat

an optimistic, positive agent who looks at the positive aspects and probes for value and benefit

the creative agent

Blue Hat

a big-picture agent that considers all the information and synthesizes the other perspectives

the big picture agent
